Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Planexa and our services

What is Planexa and how does it work?

Planexa is a comprehensive productivity platform that helps you organize your tasks, habits, and schedule in one place. It combines powerful planning tools with expert coaching to help you achieve your personal and professional goals. Our platform integrates with your favorite tools and provides actionable insights to improve your productivity.

How much does Planexa cost?

We offer several pricing plans to fit different needs:

  • Free Plan: Basic task management and limited access to resources
  • Pro Plan: $9.99/month - Advanced features and unlimited tasks
  • Business Plan: $24.99/month - Team collaboration and advanced analytics
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for organizations with specific needs

Can I use Planexa on multiple devices?

Yes! Planexa is fully responsive and works across all your devices. We offer:

  • Web application accessible from any browser
  • iOS and Android mobile apps (coming soon)
  • Desktop apps for Windows and macOS
Your data syncs automatically across all devices in real-time.

How does the coaching program work?

Our coaching program connects you with certified productivity coaches who will help you:

  • Set and achieve meaningful goals
  • Develop effective planning strategies
  • Overcome productivity challenges
  • Create sustainable habits

Coaching sessions are conducted via video call, and you can schedule them at your convenience through our platform.

Is my data secure with Planexa?

We take your privacy and data security very seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • GDPR and CCPA compliance
  • Two-factor authentication
  • Regular data backups

We never sell your data to third parties. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Click on "Billing"
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm

Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you'll continue to have access to all premium features during that time.

Do you offer discounts for students or non-profits?

Yes, we offer special discounts for students, educators, and non-profit organizations:

  • Students: 50% off any plan with a valid .edu email address
  • Educators: 30% off any plan
  • Non-profits: 40% off Business and Enterprise plans

To apply for these discounts, please contact our support team with proof of eligibility at support@planexa.com.
