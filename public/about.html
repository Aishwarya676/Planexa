Established 2025

Redefining How the World Plans.

Founded in 2025, Planexa was born from a simple mission: to bridge the gap between powerful productivity features and elegant user experience. We believe that professional tools shouldn't feel like a chore to use.

Designed and powered by the innovative team at Purple Hue Technosoft Pvt. Ltd., Planexa combines cutting-edge technology with human-centric design. We don't just build apps; we create digital companions that help you navigate your busiest days with ease.

Our platform integrates intelligent task management, seamless scheduling, and direct access to professional business coaches, making it the most comprehensive productivity ecosystem on the market.
