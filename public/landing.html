About Planexa

Planexa is your all-in-one productivity solution that helps you organize every aspect of your life. From daily tasks to long-term goals, we provide the tools you need to stay focused and achieve more.

Built with privacy in mind, Planexa stores all your data locally in your browser while offering secure cloud sync options. No ads, no data selling, just pure productivity.

  • All essentials in a single app
  • Clean UX with zero learning curve
  • Works great on desktop and mobile
  • Session-based login keeps your data safe
